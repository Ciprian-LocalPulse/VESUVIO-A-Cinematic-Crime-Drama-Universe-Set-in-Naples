# Festival & Awards Strategy

A premium series property with a completed pitch package, original
music, and a distinctive regional identity is well positioned for
festival visibility during development and post-production. Target
festivals and industry markets include:

- **Cannes (Canneseries)** — dedicated international TV series
  competition and market, a natural fit for premium European drama
  premieres.
- **Series Mania (Lille)** — Europe's leading international TV series
  festival, strong track record for regional-language crime and drama
  breakouts.
- **Venice (Venice TV Award / Biennale College)** — relevant for
  Italian-set, Italian-language premium drama with awards ambitions.
- **Berlinale Series** — strong platform for internationally-minded
  European series seeking global streaming attention.

## Strategy Notes

- Target Series Mania and Canneseries primarily during the pitch/packaging
  stage for market visibility and partner introductions.
- Target Venice and Berlinale Series for premiere placement post-
  production, contingent on the involved platform/distributor's
  premiere strategy.
- Music (original theme track) is itself a submittable/promotable asset
  independent of the series, useful for building audience awareness
  ahead of any premiere.
