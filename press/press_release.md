# FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

## "VESUVIO: Blood of Naples" — A New Mafia Saga Set Against the Naples Skyline

**Naples / 2026** — Creator Ciprian Stefan Plesca today unveiled the
complete pitch package for *VESUVIO: Blood of Naples*, an original
modern mafia television series set in the heart of Naples, Italy. The
project arrives with a completed story bible, full character bible,
Season 1 episode guide, pilot script, original Neapolitan-dialect theme
song, and cinematic key art — a fully packaged property ready for studio
and streaming partnership conversations.

"Every city has a dark heart," said creator Ciprian Stefan Plesca. "This
is Naples — and this is the story of what happens when the peace holding
that darkness in check finally breaks."

Set against the ever-present shadow of Mount Vesuvius, *VESUVIO: Blood of
Naples* follows a Neapolitan crime family navigating the collapse of a
decade-long truce, exploring loyalty, betrayal, and power across three
generations. The series is written with an uncompromising commitment to
authenticity, including dialogue performed in genuine Neapolitan dialect.

The project's original theme song — a Neapolitan-dialect duet blending
traditional motifs with dark, melodic-trap production — is available now
alongside the full pitch package.

*VESUVIO: Blood of Naples* is currently seeking production partners,
financing, and distribution conversations. Full materials are available
at the project's public repository.
