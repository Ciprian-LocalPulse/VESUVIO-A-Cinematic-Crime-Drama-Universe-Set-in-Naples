# Creator Bio — Ciprian Stefan Plesca

Ciprian Stefan Plesca is the creator of *VESUVIO: Blood of Naples*, an
original modern mafia television series concept set in Naples, Italy.
Plesca developed the project's complete creative package independently —
story concept, world-building, character bible, episode structure, pilot
script, and an original Neapolitan-dialect theme song — with the aim of
bringing an authentic, culturally specific crime saga to a global
audience.

*VESUVIO: Blood of Naples* reflects Plesca's interest in stories where
setting, language, and culture are inseparable from plot: a series that
could only take place in Naples, told the way only Naples would tell it.
