# FAQ

**Q: Is this an official Netflix or studio production?**
No. *VESUVIO: Blood of Naples* is an independently created pitch package
currently seeking production partners, financing, and distribution
conversations. See [`ROADMAP.md`](ROADMAP.md) for current status.

**Q: Can I use the story, characters, or music in my own project?**
No, not without written permission. See [`LICENSE`](LICENSE) — this is a
custom license, not standard MIT, and does not grant rights to reproduce
or adapt the creative property.

**Q: Is the dialogue really in Neapolitan dialect, not standard Italian?**
Yes. Authenticity of setting and language is a deliberate creative
choice — see [`docs/story_bible/Story_Bible.md`](docs/story_bible/Story_Bible.md),
Section 12, and the theme song lyrics in
[`docs/LYRICS_BILINGUAL.md`](docs/LYRICS_BILINGUAL.md).

**Q: Was AI used to make this?**
Generative AI tools were used as production assistants (image generation,
audio production help, documentation formatting) under full human
creative direction. See [`AI_USAGE.md`](AI_USAGE.md) for exact scope.

**Q: How can I get involved?**
See [`INVESTORS.md`](INVESTORS.md) for production/financing partnership,
or [`SUPPORT.md`](SUPPORT.md) if you'd like to contribute directly to
ongoing development.

**Q: Is there a full pilot script available?**
A sample of Act One plus a full four-act outline is included in
[`scripts/Pilot.md`](scripts/Pilot.md). The complete, production-ready
draft is available on request for serious production inquiries.

**Q: Where's the rest of the season / series?**
See [`episodes/Season_1.md`](episodes/Season_1.md) for the full Season 1
episode guide, and the Story Bible's "Series Arc" section for the
planned multi-season structure.

**Q: I'm a producer/studio — is there a more detailed FAQ for me?**
Yes — see [`PRODUCERS_FAQ.md`](PRODUCERS_FAQ.md).
