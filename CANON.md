# Canon

This document defines what is considered **canonical** (fixed, binding)
for *VESUVIO: Blood of Naples*, as distinct from material that remains
open to development (see [`LORE.md`](LORE.md) for expanded, non-binding
background material).

## Canonical Elements
- The core premise: a ten-year truce ("'a Pace") among Naples crime
  families, broken by betrayal in Season 1.
- The seven core characters and their defining relationships, as
  described in [`characters/`](characters/).
- The Season 1 episode structure and ending, as described in
  [`episodes/Season_1.md`](episodes/Season_1.md).
- The Neapolitan-dialect language commitment — dialogue is not converted
  to standard Italian or English in any canonical materials.
- The theme song and its lyrics, as the show's sonic and thematic
  signature (see [`docs/LYRICS_BILINGUAL.md`](docs/LYRICS_BILINGUAL.md)).
- Mount Vesuvius as a fixed visual and thematic motif throughout the
  series.

## Subject to Development
- Exact episode-level dialogue and scene staging beyond the Pilot sample
  (see [`scripts/Pilot.md`](scripts/Pilot.md)).
- Season 2–3 plot specifics beyond the high-level arc described in the
  Story Bible.
- Casting (no actor has been attached; casting suggestions in
  `characters/` are descriptive, not prescriptive).
- Exact budget, episode count, and platform, pending partner attachment
  (see [`BUSINESS.md`](BUSINESS.md)).

## Change Control
Any change to a canonical element listed above requires the creator's
explicit approval and should be reflected in [`CHANGELOG.md`](CHANGELOG.md).
Non-canonical / developmental material may evolve more freely during
active development conversations with attached partners.
