# Business Plan — VESUVIO: Blood of Naples

## Overview

This document outlines a preliminary business framework for developing
"VESUVIO: Blood of Naples" from pitch package to produced series. All
figures are illustrative placeholders for early conversations, not
finalized budgets — actual figures depend on attached partners,
territory, and production scale.

## Estimated Budget (Illustrative)

Premium-tier European crime drama of this scope typically budgets in the
range of **€1.5M–€3M per episode**, depending on location count, cast
tier, and VFX/production complexity. A 10-episode Season 1 would
therefore sit in an estimated **€15M–€30M** total production budget
range, subject to detailed line-producing once a partner is attached.

## Revenue Streams

- **Streaming licensing** — primary revenue path; regional-language
  crime drama has strong precedent for global streaming acquisition.
- **International distribution** — territory-by-territory licensing for
  broadcast/streaming outside the primary platform deal.
- **Soundtrack & music licensing** — the original theme and score as a
  standalone commercial release (streaming, sync licensing).
- **Merchandising** — longer-term, contingent on series success and
  brand recognition.
