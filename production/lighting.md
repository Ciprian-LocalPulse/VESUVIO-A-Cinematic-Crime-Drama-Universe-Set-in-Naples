# Production Bible — Lighting

## Core Approach

Practical-motivated, low-key lighting throughout. Naturalistic sources
(streetlamps, table lamps, candlelight in church/ritual scenes) rather
than stylized genre lighting.

## Key Locations

- **Villa interiors:** warm, low-level practicals; heavy shadow; faces
  lit unevenly to suggest concealment even within "safe" spaces.
- **Port/industrial:** hard, cold floodlight — the show's coldest,
  least forgiving lighting register.
- **Church/ritual:** candlelight and natural light through stained
  glass — the show's only consistently "soft" lighting environment.

## Vesuvius Shots

Lit only by ambient/available light at dawn, dusk, or night — never
artificially lit — preserving its role as the one unaltered, real
constant in the show's world.
