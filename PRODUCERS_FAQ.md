# FAQ for Producers & Studios

**Q: What is the current rights status of this property?**
All rights to *VESUVIO: Blood of Naples* — story, characters, title, and
associated materials — are held solely by creator Ciprian Stefan Plesca.
No option, license, or production agreement currently exists. See
[`LICENSE`](LICENSE) and [`INVESTORS.md`](INVESTORS.md).

**Q: What stage of development is this at?**
Pitch / early development. A complete package exists (story bible,
character bible, episode guide, pilot sample, original music, key art),
but no script beyond the Pilot sample has been written, and no talent,
director, or studio is currently attached. See [`ROADMAP.md`](ROADMAP.md).

**Q: What are you looking for from a production partner?**
See [`INVESTORS.md`](INVESTORS.md) — broadly: development financing,
studio/streaming partnership, and eventual production and distribution
support.

**Q: Is the dialogue really going to be in Neapolitan dialect? Will that
limit international audiences?**
Yes, dialogue authenticity is a deliberate creative choice (see Story
Bible Section 12). Regional-language crime drama has repeatedly proven
strong international streaming performance when subtitled well; this is
treated as a strength of the property, not a limitation, and is
discussed further in [`pitch/PitchDeck.md`](pitch/PitchDeck.md) ("Why
Now").

**Q: What's the intended episode count and season structure?**
Season 1 is structured as 10 episodes (~50 minutes each); a 3-season
overall arc is planned. See [`episodes/Season_1.md`](episodes/Season_1.md)
and the Story Bible's "Series Arc" section.

**Q: Can we commission a full pilot script or additional episodes before
committing?**
Yes — this is the intended next step in development financing. See
[`BUSINESS.md`](BUSINESS.md) and [`INVESTORS.md`](INVESTORS.md) for how
to start that conversation.

**Q: Who owns the original music, and can it be used independently of
the series (e.g. as a standalone release or trailer music)?**
The original theme track is part of the same creative property and
subject to the same [`LICENSE`](LICENSE); licensing it independently of
the series is possible by separate agreement — raise this directly in
your inquiry.

**Q: How do we get in touch?**
Open an issue in this repository (see the "Inquiry / Collaboration
Request" template) or refer to the contact guidance in
[`INVESTORS.md`](INVESTORS.md).
