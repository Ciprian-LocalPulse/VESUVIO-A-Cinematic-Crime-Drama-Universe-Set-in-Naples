### Notes on Dialect
The lyrics are written in authentic **Neapolitan dialect** rather than
standard Italian, reflecting the setting of the series in Naples and giving
the track linguistic and cultural authenticity consistent with the
mafia-saga genre. Phrases like *"'o rione"* (the neighborhood), *"'o
Vesuvio"* (Mount Vesuvius), and *"'o putere"* (power) are recurring motifs
tying the song directly to the show's themes of territory, loyalty, and
power.
